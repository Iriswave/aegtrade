Private Placement Program Procedure

Submission

Initial documents must include:

  • CIS/KYC with scan colour copy of passport, letter of authorization to authenticate funds and current proof of fund (POF).
  • Board Resolution (if a corporation) and Authorization to Verify (ATV) POF can be current bank statement, tear sheet, financial instrument copy and/or confirmation from the bank on its letterhead signed by bank officer(s).
  • Current Bank statement, by BCL / RWA Letter + Latest Tear Sheet + (2) Banker’s Business Cards.
  • A detailed history of how the Funds presented as Proof of Funds were earned.
Please email AEG and request the Platform authorized KYC to complete your package. We will provide a link for you to download these forms from this web page.

Verification/Authentication

After submission, compliance/security will review the documents and within 72 hours and revert back to you. Should everything be in order, the client will be contacted by AEG Trade’s intake officer to discuss the program and answer any queries. Final compliance may take 72 additional hours and on positive result, MOA will be issued.

Program Duration

Though, Standard programs are usually for 40 weeks, AEG Trade can Trade some Banking instruments or Sovereign Bonds for considerably longer. AEG will endeavour to keep the Trade ongoing in order to fund your largest infrastructure projects.


These assets can be utilized to enter the Government Trading Program:

  • Cash
  • Bank Guarantees (BG)
  • Standby Letter of Credit (SBLC)
  • Medium Term Notes (MTN)
  • Safe Keeping Receipts (Au SKR)
  • Bonds
  • Sovereign Bond
  • Bank Draft
  • MT 103 credit

Financial Instruments can be classified by whether they are cash instrument or derivative instrument. A financial instrument is a tradable asset of any kind, either cash, evidence of ownership in an entity or a contractual right to receive or deliver or any other legally enforceable financial document.

AEG Trade Inc can arrange monetization of various classes of SBLC’s, BG’s, Sovereign bonds, Bank drafts, MT 103 credits, SKR’s, (Bonds) and place the cash credits with the Private Placement Program’s Attorney Trust Trade Account. The asset owners must be able to arrange swift MT760/542/103 or other as designated from their respective Banks to the Monetization Bank or directly to the Attorney Trust Trade Account in New York, USA to initiate monetization/trade.
