Government Trade Proceeds

Projects of every imaginable size and scope can be funded with the proceeds of the Trade Platform. This will allow for debt free project funding of even the largest government projects.

Highlights

Proceeds from the trade Program are yours to use. This is not a loan; these created funds belong to you. You can build needed infrastructure and construct or modernize Ports, Roadways, develop LNG terminals and refineries, implement Agriculture improvements, and build schools and Hospitals. The list of potential acceptable projects is vast.

Every Fed Trade Program does require rigorous accounting of funds. Your intended use of these funds will be pre-approved before being accepted to into the Platform and trading begins.

Some of the world’s largest Bank Holding Companies are authorized to issue blocks of debt instruments such as medium-term notes and standby letters of credit. Essentially Government Trade Programs exist to 'create' money. Money is created by creating debt. However, this is not your debt, this is one avenue for newly created Dollars and Euros to enter the banking system and you will be the beneficiary of the new Cash generated through the Platform.

Project Finance/Loan Procedure

In some selected cases where the client does not have sufficient capital or asset availability to meet the minimum Trade entry requirements, AEG Trade may act as an asset manager on your behalf so that you can enter the Program with a smaller amount.

  • Submission: Apart from CIS/KYC, colour copy of passport, POF, a synopsis of the project (Project Profile) is required.
  • Verification/authentication: After review and verification, client will be contacted to discuss the project, equity and financial arrangements. Final compliance may take additional 2 weeks and on positive result Loan contract will be issued.
  • Disbursement term: Initial disbursement to your Trade Proceeds’ escrow account usually will be within 2 weeks after receipt of the instrument/asset. Further disbursement will be bi-monthly into your escrow account and funds disbursed as required. A Board Director will be assigned from us, who will attend board meetings, review company financial records and project completion.
